Course Overview

This course introduces the principles of designing online courses that engage and motivate learners through human-centred design and gamification. By focusing on user experience, inclusive design, and interactive content, learners will develop skills to create engaging online courses that meet diverse learner needs.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the meaning and benefits of human-centered design.
  • Understand the current limitations of online learning courses.
  • Identify the difference between traditional online course design and human-centric design.
  • Identify the conditions that lead to students taking online courses and barriers to implementing human-centered design.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the purpose of gamification.
  • Describe how to effectively implement gamification in online courses.
  • Differentiate between gamification and game-centered learning.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand what makes online content truly interactive and engaging.
  • Explain how universal design principles enhance learning.
  • Summarize WCAG standards and their importance.
  • Connect interactive features with accessibility requirements.

Learning Objectives

  • Enumerate the interpersonal and social aspects of teaching and learning.
  • Recognize the role of instructor presence, peer interaction, and creative activities in building an engaging community.
  • Apply gamified elements (e.g., badges, points) and creative thinking techniques to design inclusive community-building activities.
  • Implement moderation strategies in discussions and provide constructive feedback that encourages creativity and respects diverse perspectives.
