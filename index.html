Welcome to EDTECH 201

Human-Centred Online Learning Through Gamification

This course introduces the principles of designing online courses that engage and motivate learners through human-centered design and gamification. By focusing on user experience, inclusive design, and interactive content, learners will develop skills to create engaging online courses that meet diverse learner needs.
